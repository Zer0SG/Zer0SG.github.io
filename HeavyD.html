Our Services

Voice-Over Recording

Professional voice-over recording, editing, mixing, and mastering for commercials, videos, or podcasts. Up to 4 revisions included.

Commercial Jingles (Pre-Written)

Recording, mixing, and mastering of pre-written commercial jingles for ads, social media campaigns, and radio spots.

Full-Creation Jingles

Studio creates all music, lyrics, vocals, and production for custom jingles. Includes recording, mixing, mastering, and up to 4 revisions.

Band Recording Services

  • Live full-band tracking sessions
  • Individual instrument tracking
  • Overdubs and layering
  • EP / LP bundle deals with discounts (EP: 15%, LP: 25%)
  • Mixing & mastering included
  • Up to 4 mix revisions; additional $5 per revision

Hourly rates: Recording $35/hr, Mixing $40/track, Mastering $25/track. Deposit 35% required.
